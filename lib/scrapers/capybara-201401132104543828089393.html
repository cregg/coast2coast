    Welcome to the Coast-2-Coast Fantasy Hockey League where only one person's fantasy of winning our great championship will actually come true. The draft has been set for Sunday, September 29th at 8:15pm AST. I believe you have to be signed in by 7:30pm on that day. I'm hoping that time works for everyone as the league begins on Tuesday. All of the settings in terms of categories, player positions, etc... have been set but are subject to change before the draft. Let me know what people think they should be. We currently have the teams set at 2C 2RW 2LW 1 Utility 4D 2G 3 Bench and 1 IR. The categories are currently G, A, PIM, +/-, PPP, SOG, & Hits for skaters with W, GAA, SVP, and shutouts for goalies for a total of 11 categories.
